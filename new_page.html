PacMen Factory

Upon clicking the "start game" button in this program a pacman image will be creating in a random position travelling at a randomanized velocity across the screen. Upon reaching the edge of the page the image of the pacman will change its direction away from the wall. By utilizting the add Pacmamn button you can create as many of these pacmen as you would like.

Eye Tracker

In the center of the screen, a eye with a center pupil will change its position to look at where your cursor is on your screen.
